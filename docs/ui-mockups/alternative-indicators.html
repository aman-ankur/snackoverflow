Recommendation: Option 6 (Pill List Preview)

Why Option 6 wins: It's the only approach that actually shows what the alternatives are, not just that they exist. When users see "Steamed Rice" and "Fried Rice" pills, they immediately understand the AI's uncertainty and can make an informed decision to tap and explore further.

Options 1-5 tell users "there are alternatives" but don't explain what they are. Option 6 provides context upfront, which is more helpful for the user journey. The pills are also visually lightweight and don't feel like clutter.
